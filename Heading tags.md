here the tag `<h1></h1>` are the heading tags

there are levels in the size of the heading it goes from `1 to 6`

for example 
```html
<html>
<body>
<h1>Example</h1>
<h2>Example</h2>
<h3>Example</h3>
<h4>Example</h4>
<h5>Example</h5>
<h6>Example</h6>
</body>
</html>
```
